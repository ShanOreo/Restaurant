Our Menu

Chicken Adobo

Slow-cooked chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, and spices. Served with steamed rice.

Beef Sinigang

Tangy tamarind soup with beef, vegetables, and rice.

Lechon Kawali

Lechon Kawali is a popular Filipino pork dish deep-fried to golden perfection. Golden and crunchy on the outside, moist and flavourful on the inside.

Filipino Lumpia

Lumpia are made with paper-thin lumpia wrappers and filled with a savory mixture of ground pork, cabbage, and other vegetables.

Dinuguan

Dinuguan is a Filipino savory stew usually made of pork offal (typically lungs, kidneys, intestines, ears, heart and snout) and/or meat simmered in a rich, spicy dark gravy of pig blood, garlic, chili, and vinegar.

Pinakbet

Pinakbet includes a mix of hearty vegetables - eggplant, long green beans, bitter melon, okra, and squash or sweet potato - and your fermented seafood of choice
